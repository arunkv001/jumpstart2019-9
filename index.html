About Scholarship

The scholarship is being issued by 360DigiTMG in association with Innodatatics Inc., USA. It will be an annual scholarship program and will be open to students and working professionals. This program is our way of giving back to the student and industry community that has supported us immensely in our growth. The program offers great value on industry and career oriented courses and is available both in Malaysia and India.

FAQs

1. How can I apply for the Jumpstart Scholarship?

Fill the application form and submit it. Scholarship is open to the first 200 applicants (on a first-come-first-serve basis).

2. Does the scholarship involve writing an exam? 

Working professionals applying for the scholarship have to undergo a basic online aptitude test at our training centre. This is a 30 minute test which will have 30 questions.

3. How will I be notified if I am shortlisted for the scholarship?

Shortlisted candidates will receive an Admission Letter with payment details via email.

4. Will I get certification after finish my training?

Candidates who complete the course will receive certification.

5. I’m a student how can I claim my scholarship? 

You will be contacted if you are shortlisted. You will need to show your student ID card to confirm your scholarship offer.

6. Who is eligible to apply for the Jumpstart scholarship?

The scholarship is open to students and working professionals in Malaysia and India.

7. What subjects are the scholarships offered in?

The scholarship is offered on five popular training programs:
● Big Data for Managers
● AI Based Conversational Chat Bot Building
● Machine Learning on Cloud
● Python and R Programming
● Data Science for Internal Auditors & Accountants 

8. What does the scholarship cover?

The scholarship covers the full tuition fee. Those receiving the scholarship need only pay for certification and program deliverables.
